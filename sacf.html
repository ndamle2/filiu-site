Introduction

The Student-Athlete Community Foundation is a non-profit organization that aims to provide opportunities for student-athletes to give back to their communities.

Collaboration

This was founded by one of my mutual friends, Anirudh Murugesan, at the University of California, Davis. He’d been hosting an annual ‘New Year, New Gear’ drive that donated gently-used sports gear to kids across communities for a few years, and wanted to expand the events through an organization. He reached out over the summer to get some of the design kickstarted!
