Introduction

The Artificial Intelligence Student Collective is a child organization of Humans for AI catered towards high school and college students. It seeks to provide ethical and accessible AI literacy through pre-professional programs and events, spanning from courses at the high school-level, to symposiums at the college-level.

Collaboration

One of my friends, Neil Damle, has been working with Humans for AI for the past few years, and wanted to extend it to branches within universities, as well as surrounding high schools; he worked with Anirudh Murugesan to found AISC branches at the University of California, San Diego, as well as the University of California, Davis.
