Introduction

DormIt is a delivery service catered towards students of the University of California, San Diego, specializing in speed and late-night avalibility.

Collaboration

Because Dormit is a start-up, a lot of the roles did end up overlapping, but my main collaborator on visual design was Sunay Bargotra; other members that had given input included the UI/UX Designers that I shadowed for a bit: Cole Franklin, Emily Ton, and Jennifer Vo, as well as founders Chaninder Rishi, Eric Cherny, and Kishan Pansuria.

Experience Recap

I was relatively new to the technicalities of visual design, but I realized a lot of what I worked on contributed to building a brand, in terms of color, typography, and general direction. My time there was during the very early stages, so I worked primarily on the logo, but also involved myself in creating flyers for the beta launches.
